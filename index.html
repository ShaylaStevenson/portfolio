About Me

I am an aspiring web developer. Currently, I am studying Full Stack Web Development through the University of Washington. I have experience in HTML, CSS, Bootstrap 4, and Javascript. The skills that I am acquiring allow me to develop fully functioning, responsive web applications. I also have experience in refactoring to improve the performance of existing websites.

My background in business management provides me a unique perspective in web development. I know the frustration that comes from using a clunky and inaccessible web application. At the heart of my designs is the concept of the user's experience. I want your employees and customers to use my sites intuitivly, which is achieved by efficient flow and navigation throughout the applications.

Your website is your organization's home. As such, I want its guests to feel welcome and comfortable. Your website should represent your businesses' unique message and style. The first stage in my design process is to get to know your brand in order to create a space that supports its mission. This is achieved through the use of logical flow, informative content, cohesive color schemes, and attention-grabbing images.
